## Overview

**Maroc Concours** is a web application designed exclusively for students to help them discover and follow entrance exams for Moroccan schools, institutes, and universities.  
The platform centralizes essential information related to student competitions, making it easier to access reliable and up-to-date opportunities for continuing academic studies.

The project focuses on clarity, simplicity, and fast access to information.

---

## Key Features

- **Student-Focused Competitions**  
  Displays entrance exams related only to education and academic institutions.

- **Clear & Organized Information**  
  Each concours includes details such as institution name, field of study, eligibility conditions, and registration deadlines.

- **Search & Navigation**  
  Easy navigation to help students quickly find relevant competitions.

- **Responsive Interface**  
  Fully responsive design ensuring smooth usage on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

---

## Technologies Used

- **React.js** – Component-based front-end development  
- **Tailwind CSS** – Modern, responsive, and clean UI design  
- **JavaScript (ES6+)** – Application logic and interaction  

---

## Design & Structure

The application follows a clean front-end structure with reusable components and clear separation of concerns.  
Tailwind CSS was used to maintain consistency in layout, spacing, and typography while keeping the interface lightweight and fast.

---

## Challenges & Learnings

One of the main challenges was organizing a large amount of academic competition data in a clear and user-friendly way.  
This project helped strengthen skills in front-end architecture, UI/UX design, and building practical applications that solve real problems for students.

---

## Outcome

Maroc Concours successfully provides students with a centralized platform for academic entrance exams in Morocco.  
The project demonstrates strong front-end development skills, clean UI design, and the ability to build solutions focused on real student needs.
